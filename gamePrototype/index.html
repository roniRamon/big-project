Things to Consider

Having trouble finding out where to go? Talk to the townsfolk!

Lacking an item to advance on your journey? Investigate even the weirdest of all places! You never know what you’ll find.

Too weak to win a fight? If you’re smart enough, try outwitting them!

Not smart enough to outwit the merchant? Break his legs! Not saying the town won’t come after you, though...
